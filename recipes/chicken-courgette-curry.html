Chicken Courgette Curry

Description

This curry provides a lot of protein intake and sweetness from the courgettes

Ingredients

  • Chicken wings
  • Salt, Pepper, Smoked Paprika
  • Shallot, Garlic, Ginger, Tomates Courgettes
  • Fish sauce

Steps

  1. Fry shallot, garlic, ginger, and tomatoes until softened into a paste.
  2. Add the chicken and season with salt & pepper to taste.
  3. Add water into the pan and continue cooking on medium heat.
  4. Add courgettes half-way.
  5. Add fish sauce at the end (optional).
