Lemon Garlic Steamed Sea-bream

Description

A burmese style steamed fish. It tastes sour, spicy and refreshing!

Ingredients

  • Seam-bream
  • Garlic, Coriander, Ginger
  • Green & Red chillies, Lemon
  • Salt & Fish sauce

Steps

  1. Crush garlic, coriander, ginger, and chillies into a fine paste and add lemon juice to it.
  2. Clean & prep the fish if not already done
  3. Place the fish marinated with the paste into a steaming bowl
  4. Steam for 15 minutes
